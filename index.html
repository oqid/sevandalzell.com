About

I'm a final-year BE(Hons) Mechatronics Engineering student at the University of Auckland, finishing up papers in robotics & intelligent systems, industrial automation, and control. I like projects that sit at the join between mechanical, electrical, and code — things that have to physically work, not just compile.

Outside of coursework I shoot film and digital on Fujifilm gear, ride a GSX250R, and spend more time than is reasonable thinking about how things are put together — products, garments, robots, all the same problem really.
